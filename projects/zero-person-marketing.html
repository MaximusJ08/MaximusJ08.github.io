What I Built

I built the product as a Python Shiny application with a multi-step intake flow, server-side validation, generated strategy dashboard, and budget-aware OpenAI integration. A founder supplies core business details, constraints, tone preferences, target regions, competitors, and sensitive topics; the app normalizes that brief and passes it through a deterministic strategy-generation pipeline.

The pipeline generates personas, market intelligence, competitor positioning, UVP and messaging, channel allocation, campaign ideas, a phased roadmap, compliance review, and a logo prompt/output. It also includes feedback-driven regeneration: users can critique the strategy, the controller identifies the earliest stage to rerun, applies concrete constraints, and rechecks compliance before rebuilding the dashboard.

Project Signals

  • Python Shiny app with intake, loading, dashboard, and feedback pages.
  • Deterministic 10-stage OpenAI pipeline for strategy generation.
  • Budget tracking, validation, error handling, and compliance fix-loops.
  • Regeneration controller that turns user feedback into targeted reruns.
