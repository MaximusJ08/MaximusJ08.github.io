AI product research

Zero-person marketing company

A research and prototype project exploring whether an AI-driven multi-agent system can generate a usable, explainable, end-to-end marketing strategy for early-stage B2C startup founders.

GitHub Open report Download report Back to projects

What I Built

The report frames a zero-person marketing company as a multi-stage AI pipeline that takes minimal founder input and produces a structured marketing strategy. The system is designed around explainable AI, so each strategy item can be paired with a rationale or source instead of behaving like a black-box content generator.

The evaluation focused on usability and trust for non-expert startup users. The report found above-threshold usability in a small study, while also calling out the practical risks of generative AI strategy systems: hallucination, bias, privacy concerns, and the need to test whether generated strategies improve real outcomes.

Project Signals

  • Multi-agent LLM workflow for founder-facing marketing strategy.
  • Explainable AI layer for transparent recommendations.
  • Usability and trust evaluation with startup-oriented users.
  • Compliance and safety considerations built into the product concept.
